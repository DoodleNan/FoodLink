ABOUT THE APP

Key features of our app

Friends

Having trouble finding friends share the similar interests with food, especially when you are interested in some special kind of food? Don't worry, we would help you find them, recommending friends based on the food type you like or dislike.

Foodies

You may hate to eat alone. But with our app, you will never need to worry about that. What you need to do is very simple: open FoodLink, select the type of food you like and contact the person we recommend for you.

Discounts

Spending much money eating outside? Great news! We not only recommend friends, we also offer coupons from your favorite restaurants! Remember, the discounts are only valid when using together with your recommended friends!

Featured Restaurants

You may feel like to seek out some new restaurants. Via our app, based on your favorite food type, we will directly offer the coupons from the featured restaurants. Specific restaurants with discounts, sounds great!
